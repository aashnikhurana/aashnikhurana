# Hey, I am Aashni! 👋🏼

I'm a bioanalytical scientist with four years of experience across cGMP pharmaceutical manufacturing and academic research. I hold an MS in Chemistry from [Sam Houston State University](https://www.shsu.edu/academics/colleges/science-engineering-technology/), where my thesis on cyanide antidote protein binding was funded by the U.S. Department of Defense and the [Robert A. Welch Foundation](https://welch1.org/). I now work as a Research Scientist at [UC San Diego Health](https://health.ucsd.edu/), building an iPSC-derived motor neuron platform to study how meditation reshapes neuronal bioenergetics through plasma signaling.

I'm drawn to molecules that are difficult to measure, and to the ways AI is starting to meet life sciences.

Outside the lab, I compose music, paint, swim, play pickleball, and travel whenever the calendar allows.✨
