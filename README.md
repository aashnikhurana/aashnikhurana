# Hi there, I am Aashni! 👋


I'm an analytical and bioanalytical scientist with four years of experience across cGMP pharmaceutical manufacturing and academic research. I hold an MS in Chemistry from [Sam Houston State University](https://www.shsu.edu/academics/colleges/science-engineering-technology/) and currently work as a Research Scientist at [UC San Diego Health](https://health.ucsd.edu/). My work focuses on molecules that are difficult to measure: volatile drug candidates, neurons grown from iPSCs, and the bloodborne signals that connect mind and body. At UCSD Health, I'm building a motor neuron platform to study how meditation reshapes neuronal bioenergetics through plasma signaling.

My masters thesis on protein binding studies for cyanide antidote candidates was supported by the U.S. Department of Defense and the [Robert A. Welch Foundation](https://welch1.org/). In summer 2024, I joined a six institution research program in Hungary, funded by the College of Science and Engineering Technology at SHSU. I'm also curious about how computational and AI tools can support analytical chemistry work.

I'm currently *open to work*!
